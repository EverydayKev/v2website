Privacy Policy

The short version: we don't collect or store your personal data. If you email us, we use your message to reply — that's it.

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Everyday Technologist ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your data when you interact with our website.

2. Information Handling

The Everyday Technologist does not collect or store personal information from visitors. However, if you contact us via email or our contact form, we will have access to your name, email address, and message content solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

We kindly ask that you do not send personal or sensitive information, as we do not process or retain such data. Any personal or sensitive information received will be deleted and never shared, distributed, or sold.

3. Data Security & Protection

At The Everyday Technologist, we take privacy seriously. Since we do not collect or store user data, there is no risk of unauthorised access, data breaches, or misuse of personal information.

If you contact us, we will only retain the message temporarily to respond to your inquiry. Any personal or sensitive information sent by mistake will be deleted and never shared, distributed, or sold.

4. Sharing Your Information

The Everyday Technologist does not share, distribute, or sell any user information. As we do not collect personal data, there is no information to provide to third parties, including service providers, advertisers, or other organisations.

If you contact us, your message will remain private and will only be used to respond to your inquiry.

5. Your Rights & Control Over Information

Since The Everyday Technologist does not collect or store user data, visitors do not need to take any action to manage or delete personal information.

If you have contacted us and wish for your message to be deleted, you may simply request this and we will ensure it is removed from our records.

We do not send marketing communications or retain information for future use, so there is no need to opt out of any data collection.

6. Security Measures

At The Everyday Technologist, we take privacy seriously. Since we do not collect or store user data, there is no risk of unauthorised access, data breaches, or misuse of personal information.

Email and form inquiries are handled securely, and any personal or sensitive information sent by mistake will be deleted. We do not share data with third parties, and all communications remain private.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact The Everyday Technologist at:

info@everydaytech.com.au

We will do our best to respond promptly and address any inquiries related to privacy and data handling.
